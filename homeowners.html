Homeowners Services

As a full-service property management company, we offer complete property management services.

Our team of professionals realize your property is a valuable investment and manage your property as if it were our own. At D.E. Properties, our primary goals are to:

  • Maximize Your Cash Flow: We are committed to obtaining the maximum amount of rent for your Orange County home that market conditions will bear, thereby maximizing your income. We also work hard to minimize the cost of repairs and maintenance using our extensive network of contractors.
  • Minimize the Vacancy Period: We are committed to designing and executing a targeted marketing plan to rent your property as quickly as possible, thereby minimizing the time that your rental is not generating income.
  • Protect Your Investment: We thoroughly screen prospective tenants and manage your property in a discreet, professional, and competent manner.
  • Do the Work for You: We handle everything so you can spend your time on more important things!

Please call or E-mail us for a detailed summary of our services and fees.
